Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a custom goal? ‚ñº

To create a custom goal, go to the Goals tab and tap the "+" button. Enter your goal title, set the target count (how many times per day), choose the time interval between completions, and select a color scheme. You can create goals for any habit you want to track!

Why is my pet looking sad or unhappy? ‚ñº

Your pet's mood depends on how well you're maintaining your habits. If you haven't completed water intake, meals, or break goals recently, your pet's energy, hydration, and satisfaction will decrease. Complete your daily goals to keep your pet happy and healthy!

How do meal logging and cooldowns work? ‚ñº

The meal tracker includes 5 different meal types: Breakfast, Morning Snack, Lunch, Afternoon Snack, and Dinner. Each meal type has its own cooldown period (1.5-2 hours) to encourage proper meal spacing. You can only log each meal type once per day.

How do I view my progress analytics? ‚ñº

Go to the Progress tab to see your weekly analytics. You'll find completion rates, streaks, total completions, and detailed charts for each goal. Tap on individual goals to see more detailed breakdowns and meal-specific progress.

Can I edit or delete the default goals? ‚ñº

You can edit default goals (Water Intake, Meals & Snacks, Take Breaks, Daily Meditation) to adjust targets and intervals, but you cannot delete them. The app enforces minimum values to ensure the goals remain effective. Custom goals can be freely edited or deleted.

How do I use the meditation timer? ‚ñº

In the Daily Meditation goal, select your desired duration (1-30 minutes) and tap "Start Meditation." The app includes a breathing guide with visual cues for inhale, hold, and exhale phases. Complete the session to log your meditation and keep your pet's energy up.

How do I add photos to my journal entries? ‚ñº

When creating or editing a journal entry, tap "Add Image" in the Images section. You can select multiple photos from your photo library. Images are stored securely within the app and can be removed by tapping the X button on each image.

Will my data be synced across devices? ‚ñº

Currently, GUGU stores all data locally on your device. Your goals, progress, pet data, and journal entries are saved securely on your iPhone/iPad but don't sync across multiple devices. We're considering cloud sync for future updates.

Privacy & Data

Your Privacy Matters

GUGU is designed with privacy in mind:

  • All your data stays on your device - we don't collect or store your personal information
  • Your journal entries, photos, and goal progress are private to you
  • No analytics or tracking data is sent to external servers
  • We only use necessary permissions (camera/photos) for app functionality

For complete privacy details, please review our Privacy Policy in the App Store.
